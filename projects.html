Research Projects

A selection of my ongoing and recent research initiatives, bridging qualitative historical analysis with modern quantitative methods.

The Global Rise of Illiberalism

Examining the dismantling of liberal principles like judicial independence and academic freedom through a neo-institutional lens.

The Rise of Computational Culture

In collaboration with Liron Shani, I explore how the "big data" turn and computational thinking are reshaping scientific logic in the life sciences and humanities.

University Museums & Global Knowledge

Working with Gili Drori to study how academic knowledge is enacted publicly through university museums globally.

The "Existential Dropout"

A decade-long study using machine learning to identify why high-achieving students decide to leave academia.
