Beyond Academia

While my professional life is dedicated to sociology, I find balance through a few core passions.

Cycle Touring & Trekking

I am an avid cycle tourist and backpacker. Some highlights include a six-week solo trek in Western Mongolia and a three-month journey crossing the South Island of New Zealand on foot.

Reading

I am a dedicated reader of fiction. Recent favorites include Karel Čapek's The Gardener's Year and Haruki Murakami's Kafka on the Shore.

Cinema

I have a keen interest in cinema, particularly films that engage with human complexity. My go-to platform is Mubi, and I've recently enjoyed titles like Drive My Car and Compartment No. 6.
