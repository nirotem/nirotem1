The Lighter Side

Beyond academia, I value meaningful engagement with human complexity through physical exploration, literature, and cinema.

Expeditions & Wanderlust

On Foot & Two Wheels

I have a passion for backpacking and cycle touring. Notable journeys include a six-week solo trek in Western Mongolia and three months crossing the South Island of New Zealand on foot. These experiences sparked my interest in different cultures long before my sociological career.

Peaks & Theory

In Tajikistan, I encountered Friedrich Engels Peak. There is a Marx Peak nearby as well, which I didn't have the chance to see on that trip—perhaps a sign that foreshadowed my current sociological path.
